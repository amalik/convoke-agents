## Priority Rule (BP1)

When Strategy team design and Convoke-specific output conflict, **team design wins**. Convoke is one use case among many possible. Convoke strategic artifacts are a valuable byproduct, not the objective.

**Practical implications:**
- If a workflow step produces a great Convoke insight but doesn't generalize → redesign the step
- If an agent scope fits Convoke perfectly but would be useless for a B2B SaaS client → widen the scope
- If the best team architecture doesn't produce the specific artifact Convoke needs right now → team architecture wins
